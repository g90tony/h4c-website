Who are we?

Get to know us

Hope For Communities (H4C) is a Christian organization registered in Kenya under the Company’s Act in 2014. H4C exists to create hope in individuals and communities by empowering them to develop and emerge from their limiting situations by creating hope, vision and capacity for their own transformation. This is realized through our three core ministry pillars: namely

  • Church in Mission: Inspiring local churches for faith in action to plant, reach and transform their environment with the Good News of the Kingdom

    Church in Mission : Integrated Community Development: Facilitating individuals and communities to embrace local solutions to local challenges, e.i health, livelihoods, etc.

    Education for Life : Focusing on developing children, youth and adults for quality living through targeted educational programs.
